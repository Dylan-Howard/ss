Our Purpose

Spring Serve is an event in which Churches, Campus Ministries and Students come together to serve Bowling Green! Serving large and loving loud in Christ name here in BG!

Community Service

Community Service includes our local partnerships like Curbside, Refuge BG, Hope House and more. We also assist the elderly or widowed with service like yard clean up.

Evangelistic Outrech

Evangelistic Outreach is a key component to each service opportunity and continues to develop through each year. During 2018, many students got the opportunity to share the gospel to kids at a soccer clinic with Refuge BG.

Construction Projects

Construction Projects include the building of wheelchair ramps, repairs, railing and more. Sometimes even just a fresh coat of paint is a lot of help to an elderly person or a ministry.

Questions

Where will Spring Serve be?

This year Christ Fellowship will host Spring Serve located at 601 Graham Drive Bowling Green, KY.

Who can participate in Spring Serve?

The event is for college students, church members and leaders. Be sure to clarify on the registration form (student, leader, etc) so you can be placed appropriately.

How to I become involved in Spring Serve?

You can sign up for Spring Serve by following here.

How much does Spring Serve cost?

The event itself is free. The tee shirts will be sold for $5.

Can anyone come to Spring Serve?

Friday Night we will kick off with worship and begin our Saturday with serving. Anyone is welcome to either Friday or Saturday night's worship. Due to logistical planning Saturday is for registered participants only.

Why should I be a part of Spring Serve?

This is an opportunity to bridge WKU students with the community where are living. If a student is looking for a church home, this is a great opportunity to serve alongside other churches. This also allows students to connect with campus ministries, and meet other brothers and sisters. Bowling Green is a city that is growing fast and there are many people who need to experience the love of Christ!
